Learn Division

Division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts to get a quotient.

For example, 6 รท 3 = 2.

In this example, 6 is the dividend, 3 is the divisor, and 2 is the quotient.

The number by which another number is divided is called the divisor.

The result of division is called the quotient.
